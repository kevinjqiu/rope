=========================
 ropemacs, rope in emacs
=========================

Using rope in emacs.


Setting Up
==========

After installing pymacs, add these lines to your ``~/.emacs`` file::

  (load-library "pymacs")
  (pymacs-load "ropemacs" "rope-")
  (rope-init)


Keybinding
==========

Uses the same keybinding as in rope.
